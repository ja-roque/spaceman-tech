Our strong recommendation — start with a web app

We know you mentioned a mobile app, and we will build that. But we strongly recommend starting with a web app for Phase 1. Here is why.

Statistically, users are far more likely to engage with a platform the first time if they can open it directly in a browser. Asking someone to find your app in the App Store, download it, and create an account is a four-step barrier before they even see your product. A web app removes that completely.

On top of that, publishing on Apple and Google Play adds weeks of review time, strict content and payment policies that can delay your launch, and ongoing compliance requirements. With a web app, you launch when it is ready. Once CI has real users and real creators earning, we convert it to a mobile app in Phase 2 with a proven product and an audience already waiting.
