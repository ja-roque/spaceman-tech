1. Project Overview

Spaceman Tech LLC ("Service Provider") agrees to design, develop, and deliver CI – Creator Income, a web-based platform where creators, artists, and writers can post content, build audiences, and earn money based on views and viewer subscriptions. The client ("Client") will operate the platform as the sole administrator. This document serves as the binding agreement between both parties.

2. Scope of Work

The following features are included in this engagement:

  • Creator accounts – registration, profile setup, and content publishing (videos and posts)
  • Viewer experience – browse content, follow creators, likes, and comments
  • Video infrastructure – upload, processing, and delivery via a professional video CDN to ensure fast playback globally
  • Subscription system – viewers can subscribe to creators, with revenue distributed to creators and the platform per agreed terms
  • Earnings system – per-view and subscription-based earnings tracking for every creator, visible on their individual dashboard
  • Payout system – creators can request and receive earnings via Stripe Connect, including identity verification as required by payment regulations
  • In-app messaging – real-time direct messages between creators and users
  • Admin dashboard – full platform control including creator management, content moderation, payout approvals, subscription management, and platform analytics
  • Production deployment on a cloud server with domain and SSL configuration

Explicitly excluded from this agreement (Phase 2): Live streaming, gaming streams, ad revenue infrastructure, and native mobile applications for iOS and Android. These will be scoped separately after Phase 1 delivery.
